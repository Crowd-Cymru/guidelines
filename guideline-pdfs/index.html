About Edward Thomas

Philip Edward Thomas (1878-1917) was a British poet, essayist, and novelist. Though born in London, he is closely associated with the countryside of England and Wales. He is considered one of the most important poets of the early 20th century, known for his nature poetry and his tragic death in World War I at the Battle of Arras. This archive preserves his literary legacy for future generations.
